About TEART

What is TEART?

Teart is a upcoming digitalized art brand that creates creative digital art potraits,customized graphic designs and wall arts for beautifying the interior of houses

Inspiration

Teart is "tech plus art" created by Katso Kgosietsile Sekhute who goes by the stage name "DimaKatso", a 19 year old boy, who currently enrolls a full time tech programme in BAC. DimaKatso discovered his talent and passion for art in C.A.P.A(Creative Arts and Performing Arts) and continued with the same subject, perfoming exceptionally well in Nanogang C.J.S.S and St Josephs college.

Our Mission

Teart aims to blend technology and art together in order to display beautiful pieces that will remain timeless for each customer

Art Style

Teart uses ibis x paint and sketchbook to create digital art pieces buy mainly using airbrush, glowing and pixelated brushes
